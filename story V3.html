How It All Started

It all started with just 7 friends who shared a strong enthusiasm for cars. We spent hours discussing changes, dream rides, and events we wanted to attend but couldn't find. That's when we decided to launch Shop07, a company that would provide vehicle lovers like us with the enhancements they required while also creating a community based on our common love of vehicles.

Shop07 is more than just a business; it's a community of automotive enthusiasts. We make one-of-a-kind car modifications and hold events and meetings where people can show off their projects, swap ideas, and celebrate the automobile culture that we all appreciate. If you want to upgrade your ride or meet other enthusiasts, Shop07 is the place to go.
